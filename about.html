About minimalie

minimalie is a minimalist Linux distribution based on Linux Mint, featuring the KDE / Plasma desktop environment. Experience simplicity and elegance in one package.

What is minimalie?

minimalie is a Linux distribution that is designed to be as simple and lightweight as possible. It is based on Linux Mint, but it has been stripped down of all the unnecessary bloat. This makes minimalie a great choice for users who want a fast and responsive operating system.

minimalie also features a beautiful and elegant desktop environment. The KDE Plasma desktop is known for its customizability, and minimalie takes advantage of this to offer a truly unique experience.

Features

  • Simplicity: minimalie is designed to be as simple and easy to use as possible.
  • Lightweight: minimalie is a very lightweight operating system, which makes it perfect for older computers.
  • Customizability: the KDE Plasma desktop is highly customizable, so you can create a look that is perfect for you.
  • Security: minimalie is a secure operating system, with features like full disk encryption and app sandboxing.
  • Community: the minimalie community is friendly and helpful, and they are always willing to offer support.
